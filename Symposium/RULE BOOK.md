# Blind Coding Challenge

**See the bug. Think the fix. No trial. No error.**

**Description:**  
Test your true coding skills by debugging programs without running them. This challenge evaluates your logical thinking and ability to identify and fix errors through pure analysis.

**Uniqueness:**  
Unlike traditional coding contests, participants cannot rely on compiler feedback. This event focuses entirely on logical reasoning and debugging expertise.

**Team Size:**  
• Individual or Team of 2 members

**Platform:**  
• HackerRank

**Rules:**  
• Participants must debug and submit corrected code.  
• Use of mobile phones, internet, or AI tools is strictly prohibited.  
• Any malpractice will lead to disqualification.  
• Judge’s decision will be final.

**Constraints:**  
• Duration: 60–90 minutes  
• 4–6 debugging questions  
• Languages allowed: C, C++, Java, Python

**Process:**  
• Participants receive HackerRank contest link.  
• Identify and correct errors in given programs.  
• Submit corrected solutions.  
• Winners decided based on score and submission time.

# Tech Escape Room

**Hack the clues. Crack the code. Escape the room.**

**Description:**  
An immersive real-world technical escape challenge where participants solve cryptographic and logical puzzles using physical objects to unlock a secured laptop. The laptop contains the final boss program, which must be solved to obtain the escape key.

**Uniqueness:**  
Participants interact with real physical objects, decode cryptographic clues, unlock a real laptop, and defeat a final coding challenge to escape. This creates a thrilling real-world hacking simulation experience.

**Team Size:**  
• Team of 3–4 members

**Platform:**  
• Offline Physical Setup (Locked Laptop + Local Program)

**Rules:**  
• Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.  
• Teams must solve challenges using only provided materials.  
• Tampering with equipment is not allowed.  
• Teams must follow the challenge sequence.  
• Judge’s decision will be final.

**Constraints:**  
• Duration: 45–60 minutes  
• 3 real-world cryptographic and logical challenges  
• 1 final boss coding challenge on locked laptop  
• Only provided system may be used

**Process:**

**Stage 1 – Cryptographic Puzzle**  
• Teams receive encrypted message (Caesar cipher, Binary, QR clue, or substitution cipher).  
• Solving it reveals the password hint.

**Stage 2 – Physical Object Challenge**  
• Teams locate clues hidden in physical objects (keyboard, printed sheets, hardware components, posters).  
• Final answer unlocks the laptop password.

**Stage 3 – Laptop Unlock**  
• Teams enter correct password to unlock laptop.  
• Laptop contains the final boss program.

**Final Boss Challenge – Escape Program**  
• Teams must solve the final coding or logic program.  
• Correct solution reveals escape key.  
• Escape key unlocks the room.

**Winning Criteria:**  
• First team to escape wins.  
• Fastest completion time determines ranking.

# Algorithm Auction

**Bid smart. Code smarter. Win bigger.**

**Description:**  
Participants bid on algorithm problems using virtual money and solve them strategically to maximize their score and profit.

**Uniqueness:**  
Combines coding with strategic bidding, testing both problem-solving skills and decision-making ability.

**Team Size:**  
• Team of 2 members

**Platform:**  
• HackerRank

**Rules:**  
• Each team receives virtual money.  
• Teams must bid to select problems.  
• Solving problems earns points.  
• Incorrect or unsolved problems may result in loss.

**Constraints:**  
• Auction duration: 30 minutes  
• Coding duration: 90 minutes  
• Fixed virtual budget for all teams

**Process:**  
• Teams participate in auction round.  
• Selected problems unlocked in HackerRank.  
• Teams solve problems.  
• Winner based on highest score.
